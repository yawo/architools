## Medusa

**Repository:** [medusajs/medusa](https://github.com/medusajs/medusa)
**Description:** The world's most flexible open-source commerce platform and framework for building custom digital commerce applications.
**Key Features:**
- Modular commerce building blocks (Products, Orders, Customers, etc.)
- Built-in framework for extensive customization and extension
- Support for complex setups: B2B, DTC, Marketplaces, and PoS systems
- Advanced promotional engine and price lists
- Multi-region and multi-currency support
**Primary Use Cases:** Custom eCommerce stores, marketplaces, subscription services, and headless commerce.
**Tags:** #ecommerce #framework #typescript #headless
**Added:** 2026-05-24
**Source:** [GitHub](https://github.com/medusajs/medusa)

### Notes / Why Notable
Medusa is often cited as the open-source alternative to Shopify, offering significantly more flexibility for developers. Its modular architecture allows businesses to pick and choose the commerce features they need without being locked into a rigid platform.
